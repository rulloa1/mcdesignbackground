# Build Brief: Michael Chandler — Strategic Construction Executive

## High-Level Vision
Elevate the current "Michael Chandler" portfolio from a high-end curiosity to a high-performance **Conversion Engine** for ultra-luxury residential and civil contracts. We will maintain the "Cinematic" feel of the scroll-linked canvas but surround it with authoritative, process-driven content derived from elite competitor patterns.

---

## 1. Content Strategy
- **"The Chandler Process":** Instead of just listing services, we will create a 3-step visualization of Michael’s methodology:
    1. **Pre-Construction & Discovery** (Planning, land advisory, budgeting)
    2. **Integrated Execution** (Design-build, $500M+ precision)
    3. **Legacy Delivery** (Post-handover support, generation-grade quality)
- **Authority Credentials:** Promote the "37 Years" and "$500M+ Portfolio" stats from plain text to dynamic, interactive counters.
- **Consultative CTAs:** Transition all buttons from "Get in Touch" to **"Request a Project Discovery Call"** or **"Discuss Your Portfolio."**

## 2. Visual Style & UI
- **Refining the Palette:** Stick to the custom `Bone` (#F4F0E8) and `Onyx` (#1C1916) theme.
- **Typed Micro-interactions:** Add a sense of "technical precision" by having certain labels or stats reveal themselves with a typing effect as you scroll.
- **Glassmorphism Panels:** Use semi-transparent, frosted-glass panels for the service cards to maintain the cinematic background visibility.
- **Enhanced Mobile Nav:** A dedicated, full-screen menu with large typography and social/contact shortcuts.

## 3. SEO & Conversion
- **Niche Focus:** Explicitly target "Ultra-Luxury Custom Homes," "Historic Coastal Restorations," and "Design-Build Construction Management."
- **Localized Footprint:** Add a "Regions Served" footer component listing California, Bahamas, Texas, and Montana—key high-net-worth migration hubs.
- **The "High-End Friction" Form:** A multi-step lead form (built with Vanilla JS) that asks for "Project Budget Range" and "Project Type" to pre-qualify inquiries.

## 4. Technical Stack
- **Core:** Vanilla HTML5 / Tailwind CSS (CDN)
- **Animation:** GSAP (Core) + ScrollTrigger (for the 294-frame sequence and new text reveals).
- **Performance targets:**
    - Lighthouse Score: 95+ (Performance, Accessibility, SEO).
    - Canvas optimization: Efficient frame loading to prevent flickering.

---

## 5. Execution Timeline
1. **Refine Hero:** Clean up the canvas loading logic.
2. **Implement Process Section:** Insert the new 3-step process map.
3. **Upgrade Portfolio:** Enhance the filtering logic for civil vs. residential.
4. **Final Polish:** Accessibility audit and conversion testing.
