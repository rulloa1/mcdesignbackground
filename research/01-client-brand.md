# Brand Extraction: Michael Chandler

## Brand Snapshot

- **Company:** mcdesign / Michael Chandler
- **Primary Color:** `#1C1916` (Dark Charcoal)
- **Secondary Color:** `#F4F0E8` (Warm Bone)
- **Accent Color:** `#6B6660` (Stone Gray)
- **Fonts:** Inter (Heading & Body)
- **Tone:** Authoritative, Minimalist, Craft-focused
- **Core Message:** 37 Years. Built by Hand. $500M+ portfolio.

---

## Detailed Extraction

### 1. Visual Identity

- **Logo:** Simple SVG mark (geometric squares) with "mcdesign" text.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Background: `var(--bg)` -> `#F4F0E8`
  - Text: `var(--text)` -> `#1C1916`
  - Muted: `var(--text-muted)` -> `#6B6660`
  - Buttons: Black (`#111`) / Transparent with outline.
- **Typography:** Google Fonts: `Inter`. Weight variants: 300 (light), 400 (regular), 700 (bold), 900 (black).

### 2. Tone & Messaging

- **Voice:** Direct, experienced, and high-performance. Avoids flowery language in favor of hard numbers ($500M+, 37 years).
- **Key Headlines:**
  - "37 Years. Built by Hand."
  - "Uncompromising Precision."
  - "Built to Last Generations."
  - "Exceptional results come from exceptional teams."

### 3. Core content

- **Services:**
  - Design-Build (Integrated delivery)
  - Construction Management (Owner-rep)
  - Civil & Site Work (Coastal restoration, infrastructure)
- **Portfolio:** Deep portfolio including Residential Construction, Design Build, Civil, Hospitality, and Development. Locations spanning California, Bahamas, Texas, Montana, Utah, and Florida.
