## Patterns of the Top 10%

1. **Integrated "Process" Visualizer:** High-end firms sell the *experience* of building as much as the house. Every top site has a dedicated "Our Process" or "The Design-Build Advantage" section explaining the single-source accountability.
2. **Consultative Lead Capture:** Rather than just a "Contact Us" form, top firms use a "Complimentary Consultation" or "Project Discovery" approach to lower the friction for high-net-worth clients.
3. **Awards & Credentials as Trust Signals:** Displaying logos for *Houzz*, *Chrysalis*, or regional architecture awards increases perceived value significantly.
4. **Service Area Precision:** Using sub-pages or sections for "Custom Homes in [City Name]" helps capture high-intent local search traffic.

## Recommended Design Direction (Michael Chandler)

- **Preserve:** The premium black/beige aesthetic and the frame-based GSAP scroll animation.
- **Enhance:** 
    - **"The Chandler Process":** A 3-step visual guide (Vision -> Execution -> Legacy).
    - **Consultative CTA:** Change "Get in Touch" to "Request a Project Discovery Call".
    - **Social Proof:** Transition the stats into a more interactive "Impact" section with quotes.
    - **Filtering:** Improve the portfolio filter UX for mobile.
